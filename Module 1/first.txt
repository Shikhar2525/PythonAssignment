Q.1  a= 4*3/4+4/4+8-2+5/8

Ans. 12/4+4/4+8-2+5

     1. Precedence of the operator is from
          left --> right
     2. Execution of an operator is done as :
          * /
          + -

	a=4*3/4+4/4+8-2+5/8
	a=12/4+4/4+8-2+5/8
	a=3+4/4+8-2+5/8
	a=3+1+8-2+5/8
	a=3+1+8-2+0.625
	a=4+8-2+0.625
	a=12-2+0.625
	a=10+0.625
	a=10.625
